About Us

The Department of Transportation is responsible for ensuring safe and efficient transportation systems. We strive to provide excellent service to our community.

Government Offices

Visit our government offices to access additional services and assistance:

  • Transportation Licensing Office
  • Vehicle Registration Office
  • Traffic Safety Department
  • Road Maintenance Office

Our knowledgeable staff at these offices can help you with various transportation-related matters, including:

  • Issuing driver's licenses and ID cards
  • Processing vehicle registrations and permits
  • Providing information on traffic safety regulations
  • Handling road maintenance and repair requests
